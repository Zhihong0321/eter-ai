# JinkoSolar Tiger Neo 3.0: High-Efficiency Solar Panels for Malaysian Climates

## JinkoSolar Tiger Neo 3.0

The JinkoSolar Tiger Neo 3.0 represents the latest generation of N-type Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) photovoltaic modules, optimized for tropical equatorial climates like Malaysia. Built on Jinko's proprietary HOT 4.0 platform, this series delivers industry-leading efficiency and reliability for Malaysian residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar installations.

## Key Features

### HOT 4.0 Technology Platform
The Tiger Neo 3.0 incorporates three core innovations at the cell level:

- **Metallization Enhancement (ME) Technology** – Optimizes the metal-semiconductor interface, reducing contact resistance and parasitic absorption while boosting absolute cell efficiency by up to 0.5%
- **Half-Cut Passivation (HCP) Technology** – Applies a protective passivation layer to all laser-cut edges of half-cut cells, minimizing recombination losses at cell boundaries
- **Jinko MAX Technology** – Optimizes the internal rear-side structure for improved charge carrier transport and reduced internal heat accumulation

### Advanced Cell Architecture
- **N-type TOPCon 7 cell technology** – Superior to legacy P-type PERC modules
- **Multi-segment 264-cell design** – Enhanced shade tolerance
- **Smart Multi-Busbar (SMBB) interconnection** – Reduces resistive losses

## Advantages for Malaysian Conditions

### Thermal Performance
Malaysia's rooftop solar installations regularly experience operating cell temperatures of 45–65°C under peak irradiance. The Tiger Neo 3.0's temperature coefficient of **-0.29%/°C** significantly reduces power loss compared to legacy PERC modules:

- At 65°C operating temperature: approximately **8.7% loss** (vs. 10.5–11.6% for PERC)
- Lower operating temperatures extend module lifespan and improve annual energy yield

### Humidity and PID Resistance
The Tiger Neo 3.0 addresses Malaysia's high humidity (averaging 81% RH) through:

- **Dual-glass packaging** with POE (polyolefin elastomer) encapsulation – superior water vapor resistance compared to standard EVA
- **Dense silicon nitride (SiNx) anti-reflective coating** – acts as a physical barrier to sodium ion migration, preventing Potential-Induced Degradation (PID)
- **N-type silicon doping with phosphorus** – immune to Boron-Oxygen complex degradation (LID/LeTID)

### Low-Light Performance
Frequent monsoonal cloud cover reduces irradiance to diffuse light levels around 200 W/m². The Tiger Neo 3.0 maintains **96.77% efficiency at 200 W/m²**, outperforming competing back-contact (BC) modules that drop to 93–95% under the same conditions.

### Shade Mitigation
The **multi-segment cell design with 3 bypass diodes** isolates only shaded segments rather than bypassing entire strings:

- Maintains **91.67% of power output** under partial shading (vs. 83.33% for standard modules)
- **TÜV Rheinland Class A+ Anti-Shading Certification**
- Quarter-cut cell technology reduces resistive power losses
