## All-in-One Energy Storage Systems

### HS2 Series – First Generation

The HS2 integrates the Power Conversion System (PCS), Battery Management System (BMS), and stackable high-voltage battery modules in an IP65 outdoor-rated cabinet.

**Specifications:**
- **AC Power Range:** 3 kW – 10 kW (Single/Three Phase)
- **String Current:** 16 A
- **Battery Capacity:** 5.0 kWh – 25.0 kWh (1-5 modules)
- **Usable Capacity:** 4.5 kWh per module (90% DoD)
- **Backup Transfer:** <10 ms UPS switchover
- **Operating Temperature:** -10°C to 50°C

**Features:**
- AC retrofit capability for existing solar systems
- Supports Type III surge protection
- Compatible with BYD and SAJ B2 battery stacks

### HS3 Series – Next Generation

The HS3 introduces significant hardware and mechanical upgrades over the HS2.

**Specifications:**
- **AC Power Range:** 3 kW – 12 kW (Single/Three Phase)
- **String Current:** 20 A
- **Battery Capacity:** 5.0 kWh – 40.0 kWh (1-8 modules via BC3 combiner)
- **Usable Capacity:** 4.5 kWh per module (90% DoD)
- **Physical Depth:** Ultra-slim 170 mm
- **Operating Temperature:** -20°C to 55°C with integrated heating

**Key Upgrades:**
- **Module-Level DC-DC Optimization:** Each 5.0 kWh pack operates independently, preventing capacity mismatching and allowing mixed-age module stacking
- **Self-Heating Elements:** Enables outdoor operation in cold climates down to -20°C
- **Flexible Expansion:** Up to 3 modules per tower; 4-8 modules via BC3 battery combiner box
- **Cable-Free Design:** Clean internal busbar connections

## Warranty Information

**Standard Warranty Terms:**
- **Duration:** 10 years (120 months) for BU2, B2, AS2, and HS2 series
- **Capacity Guarantee:** Minimum 60% of nominal capacity (70% in Australia)

**Critical Warranty Conditions:**
- **Throughput Cap:** Warranty is void if the battery exceeds specified total energy throughput (calculated at approximately 3,060 full cycles in Australia)
- **Online Registration Required:** System must be registered and maintain internet connection
- **Offline Penalty:** If unregistered or offline, warranty reduces to 5.5 years maximum from shipment date
- **Firmware Updates:** OTA updates delivered via SAJ portal for safety monitoring

**Important Sizing Note:** For households with aggressive daily cycling, TOU tariff arbitrage, or VPP participation, the throughput limit may be reached in under 8.5 years. Consider larger capacity systems to reduce daily depth of discharge.
