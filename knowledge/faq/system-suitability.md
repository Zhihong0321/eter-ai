# Is This System Suitable for My Property?

The proposed system should only be confirmed after it is matched to your property and electricity use. Our process reviews historical bills and load profile, then checks roof orientation, structural condition, shading, and electrical-board requirements during the technical site assessment. Those findings determine the safe mounting approach and appropriate system capacity. If the assessment changes an assumption, we revise the design rather than force the original proposal onto your property.

## Supporting Facts

- Technical Site Assessment covers roof orientation, structural integrity, and shading (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- Domestic capacity limits are supported up to 5 kWac (single-phase) or 15 kWac (three-phase) (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- System sizing is strictly based on historical load profile and bill analysis (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
