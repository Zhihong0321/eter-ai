# Who Will Handle My Installation and Support?

Your project stays with one accountable Eternalgy team. Our self-operated delivery team handles the installation, supported by in-house roof specialists rather than an unmanaged chain of subcontractors. After commissioning, you retain mobile system monitoring, technical support available six days a week, and annual maintenance checkups. That continuity makes it easier to get clear answers and timely support throughout the system's life.
