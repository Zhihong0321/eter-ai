# What Protection and Warranty Will I Receive?

Your proposal includes several layers of protection, each covering a different risk. Eternalgy's standard package lists a 12-year JinkoSolar product warranty, a 30-year linear power warranty, a 10-year SAJ string-inverter product warranty, a 3-year workmanship warranty, and a 1-year roof-leak warranty. It also includes three years of MSIG all-risk solar-system insurance. The final protection depends on the exact equipment and terms in your signed proposal, which our team will walk through with you before acceptance.

## Supporting Facts

- JinkoSolar Panels: 12-Year Product Warranty + 30-Year Linear Power Warranty (Source: `knowledge/company/eternalgy-company-overview.md`)
- SAJ String Inverters: 10-Year Product Warranty (Sole distributor coverage) (Source: `knowledge/company/eternalgy-company-overview.md`)
- Eternalgy Installation: 3-Year Workmanship Warranty + 1-Year Roof Leaking Warranty (Source: `knowledge/company/eternalgy-company-overview.md`)
- MSIG Solar Insurance: 3 Years of all-risk coverage for the entire system (Source: `knowledge/company/eternalgy-company-overview.md`)
