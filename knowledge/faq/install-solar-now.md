# Why Should I Install Solar Now?

Installing in 2026 may unlock incentives that are time-limited or allocation-limited, subject to eligibility. For eligible residential Solar ATAP systems, SuRIA Home offers RM 600 per kWac up to RM 3,000 on a first-come, first-served basis until 31 December 2026 or until the allocation is exhausted, whichever comes first. Qualifying businesses may also consider GITA for eligible expenditure incurred through 31 December 2026. The practical next step is to check your eligibility and project timeline now—without assuming any incentive is guaranteed until approval and commissioning requirements are met.

## Supporting Facts

- SuRIA Home Programme direct cash rebate capped at RM 3,000, deadline Dec 31, 2026 (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- GITA allows 60% tax allowance on qualifying solar CAPEX, valid until Dec 31, 2026 (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- Official SuRIA Home conditions: [SEDA Malaysia](https://www.seda.gov.my/government-introduced-sustainable-rebate-incentive-assistance-suria-home/)
