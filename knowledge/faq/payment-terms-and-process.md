# What Are the Payment Terms and Installation Process?

You only act at three milestones — 5% deposit, 60% before installation, and 35% after installation is complete. Eternalgy handles all government applications and paperwork in between. Your system is installed within weeks of approval, and you pay the final amount only once you are satisfied with the work.

## Supporting Facts

- Step 1: 5% deposit to confirm order
- Step 2: SEDA Solar ATAP application and approval (4–8 weeks)
- Step 3: 60% payment due before installation begins
- Step 4: Installation scheduled and completed (2–4 weeks)
- Step 5: Remaining 35% full payment after installation is confirmed complete
- Step 6: TNB Smart Meter change application submitted
- Step 7: TNB activates the ATAP export meter (2–4 weeks)
- Step 8: Solar ATAP system fully activated and generating
