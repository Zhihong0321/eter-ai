# Why Was This Solar Panel Selected?

We selected the JinkoSolar Tiger Neo 3.0 to perform well in Malaysia's heat, humidity, cloud cover, and constrained roof space. Its N-type TOPCon design has a -0.29%/°C temperature coefficient, while dual-glass construction improves resistance to moisture. Its multi-segment design also limits losses from partial shading. JinkoSolar's reported 100% score in BloombergNEF's 2024 bankability survey and its 30-year linear power warranty add long-term confidence without promising a fixed energy yield.

## Supporting Facts

- Tiger Neo 3.0 temperature coefficient of -0.29%/°C significantly reduces power loss at operating temperatures of 45–65°C (Source: `knowledge/products/jinko-tiger-neo-3-overview.md`)
- Dual-glass packaging provides superior water vapor resistance (Source: `knowledge/products/jinko-tiger-neo-3-overview.md`)
- JinkoSolar achieved a 100% bankability rating, ranking #1 globally (Source: `knowledge/products/jinko-tiger-neo-3-overview.md`)
- 30-year linear power warranty (Source: `knowledge/products/jinko-tiger-neo-3-overview.md`)
