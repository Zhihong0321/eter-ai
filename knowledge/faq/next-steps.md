# What Is the Next Step?

To proceed, confirm that you would like our team to review the final proposal and acceptance requirements with you. We will verify the system scope, warranty terms, site-assessment status, and any financing needs before submission. For Solar ATAP, a SEDA-registered PV Service Provider submits the application through eATAP using the required customer, property, technical, and TNB documents. After the necessary approvals and site conditions are confirmed, our delivery team coordinates the installation and commissioning schedule with you.

## Supporting Facts

- Applications must be submitted through SEDA's eATAP portal via a SEDA-registered PV Service Provider (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- Online submission via eATAP requires applicant's profile, MyKad, and latest TNB bill (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
- Green financing options are available, such as Maybank Green Loan and CIMB Green Loan (Source: `knowledge/schemes/solar-atap-malaysia-complete-guide.md`)
