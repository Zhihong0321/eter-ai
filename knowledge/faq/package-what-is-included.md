# What Is Included in the Solar Package?

Your complete rooftop solar package includes 11 × 650W JinkoSolar TIGER NEO 3.0 N-type TOPCon panels and 1 × 3-phase SAJ R6 5kW string inverter as the core equipment. The package also covers SEDA ATAP application support and TNB smart meter application, so all government and grid paperwork is handled for you. Design and installation services include solar system architecture design, electrical system design, roof and site surveying, roof panel installation, electrical works, and SkyLift access equipment. Every installation carries full layered protection: JinkoSolar panels carry a 12-year product warranty and 30-year linear power warranty; the SAJ inverter carries a 10-year product warranty; Eternalgy provides a 3-year workmanship warranty and 1-year roof-leak warranty; and the entire system is covered by 3-year MSIG all-risk insurance from day one.

## Supporting Facts

- Panel brand: JinkoSolar TIGER NEO 3.0 — N-type TOPCon, 24.8% efficiency, -0.29%/°C temperature coefficient
- Inverter brand: SAJ R6 5kW — 3-phase, 98.2% peak efficiency, built-in Type II DC surge protection
- SEDA ATAP: Malaysia's solar grid-export scheme (replaced NEM 3.0 on 1 Jan 2026); Eternalgy handles the full eATAP application
- TNB smart meter: Required for net-billing; application submitted by Eternalgy
- SkyLift: Motorised access equipment for safe rooftop panel installation
