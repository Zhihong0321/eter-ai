# Why Is This Proposal Worth the Price?

A solar proposal should be compared by lifetime value, not price alone. Our proposal combines durable equipment, engineering safety margins, an in-house delivery team, roof expertise, and clearly defined warranty and insurance protection. These inclusions reduce the risks that matter over a system life of 20 years or more: poor workmanship, unclear accountability, avoidable downtime, and difficult warranty support.
