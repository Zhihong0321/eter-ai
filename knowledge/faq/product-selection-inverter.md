# Why Was This Inverter Selected?

Your inverter is the control centre of the solar system, converting panel output into usable electricity and reporting system performance. The proposed SAJ string-inverter range offers high conversion efficiency, quiet operation, integrated surge protection on applicable models, and app-based monitoring. Eternalgy's standard package lists a 10-year product warranty for the proposed SAJ string inverter. We will confirm the exact model, protection features, and warranty terms shown in your proposal before you proceed.

## Supporting Facts

- SAJ R5 series features ultra-quiet operation (<29 dB) and integrated Type III surge protection (Source: `knowledge/products/saj-inverter-battery-product-range.md`)
- SAJ inverters connect with the elekeeper Smart EMS for real-time monitoring (Source: `knowledge/products/saj-inverter-battery-product-range.md`)
- Eternalgy is the SAJ Inverter Sole Distributor of Malaysia (Source: `knowledge/company/eternalgy-company-overview.md`)
