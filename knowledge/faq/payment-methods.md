# What Payment Methods Are Available?

Eternalgy accepts three payment methods: Online GIRO / Cash Deposit, Visa/Mastercard Credit Card (full payment), and Credit Card Easy Payment Plan (EPP) for monthly instalments through selected banks. EPP lets you spread the cost over 6 to 60 months depending on your bank. Available banks and indicative annual interest rates are listed below.

## Supporting Facts

- Accepted payment: Online GIRO, Cash Deposit, Visa/Mastercard Credit Card
- EPP banks: Maybank, Public Bank, Hong Leong Bank, OCBC, UOB, AmBank, CIMB
