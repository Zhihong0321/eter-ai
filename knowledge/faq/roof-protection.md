# What Happens to My Roof?

We treat the roof as part of the solar system—not as an afterthought. Our technical assessment checks roof condition and structural suitability before installation, while our in-house roof specialists can address identified repairs or reinforcement needs. The mounting plan is then designed around the property's actual condition. Eternalgy's standard package also includes a 1-year roof-leak warranty, subject to the signed proposal terms.
